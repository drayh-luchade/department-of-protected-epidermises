Times shown in UTC (Z); your local equivalent is listed below each button (a "prev day"/"next day" note appears if that hour actually falls on a different local calendar date than the T-3…T+3 day selected above — since each day here is a UTC calendar date, its first and/or last hour can spill into your local yesterday or tomorrow). Hours marked with a green underline have already occurred and reflect recorded conditions — hours without it are still model forecasts for later today and may change before they arrive.

About the Department of Protected Epidermises

The National Lotion Service is the flagship weather product of the Department of Protected Epidermises (DOPE), a fictional cabinet-level agency dedicated to the earnest, thorough, and entirely unnecessary monitoring of American skin.

Disclaimer: The National Lotion Service is a fictional agency. This index is a humorous visualization based on real environmental data and is not intended as medical advice.

Data sourced from NASA POWER, USGS, and Census TIGER. Updated automatically every 6 hours.
